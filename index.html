PRISM: Proxy Recognition and Inclusive Scoring Method

Evaluating Context-Dependent Bias in Large Language Models for Resume Screening

This website accompanies our research poster presented at the Fall 2024 Computing Showcase. PRISM is a framework for analyzing how large language models respond to demographic proxy cues and how prompt framing influences bias expression.
